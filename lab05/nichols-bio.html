Yaneeka Nichols Biography

Hello, my name is Yaneeka Nichols. I am a Junior undergraduate at Syracuse University, majoring in Information Management & Technology with a double concentration in Web Design & Management and Innovation, Design & Startups, as well as a minor in Public Communications Studies. My primary interests include web design for designer brands and human-interface design for private sector tech companies. I’ve gained experience interning as a web designer for the US Army War College’s CMRC program and designing websites for family businesses, including real estate and graphic design ventures.

Personal Information

Outside of my career and academics, I enjoy traveling, reading, and creating art. I love exploring new places and keep detailed lists of where I’ve been and where I hope to visit, which helps me plan my next adventures. My favorite author is Stephen King, though I also enjoy books that explore life lessons and morals. When it comes to art, I find joy in canvas painting and coloring.

Goals & Aspirations

After graduation, I aim to work in web design for designer fashion brands such as Louis Vuitton, Chanel, or Burberry, or to join Apple’s Human Interface Design team, with aspirations of growing into a leadership position. Beyond my professional goals, I am always eager to seek new opportunities to expand my knowledge—both within my field of study and beyond—while making meaningful contributions to the projects I undertake.
